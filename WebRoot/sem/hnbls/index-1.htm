Company Profile
Hunan Bonanza Valve Co. LTD, founded in May, 2007, is a high-tech enterprise specialized in producing water valves, strobe, water pump, trash rack and some other hydraulic machinery. With a staff of 166 people, our company is located at Ningxiang (a county-level city of Changsha in Hunan province) Economic and Technological Development Zone. It is equipped with a brand-new manufacturing workshop of 30,000 m2 and various advanced production equipment and it has a great number of high quality personnel in technology and manufacturing management. 21 patents are attained by our company, with new products under research. The main product is "Energy-saving Side Automatic Devices for Anti-reflux" used for flood storage, city self-drainage and inter basin water transfer, making it a best substitute for the traditional flap valve and lock gate. This technology was awarded the second-scale prize of "Dayu Hydro-scientific Technology Prize" in October, 2007 and was included in 948 Key Expansion Project under Ministry of Water Resources in May, 2008. In January, 2009, this technology was brought into "Instructive Catalog for Intensive Promotion of Advanced and Practical Technologies in Water Conservancy Project in 2008". Our products are popular with the clients in Hunan, Hubei, Jiangxi, Heilongjiang, Guangdong, Henan, Sichuan, Gansu and so on.

2. Energy-saving Side Automatic Devices for Anti-reflux
2.1 Energy-saving Side Automatic Devices for Anti-reflux is proprietary product with a patent owned by Hunan Bonanza Valve Co. LTD. The first generation of this product was called "Energy-saving Valve for Flood Storage in Irrigation and Drainage " and the second generation was called "Free Energy-saving Rollover-type Flap Valve". Energy-saving Side Automatic Devices for Anti-reflux is the third generation and it consists of the following two kinds:
  • Energy-saving Side Flap Valve
  • Energy-saving Self Valve
2.2 Working principle
Based on principle of mechanics, if you vertically lay the door axis and tilts it to a certain angle, you can open or close the flap valve with the pressure difference between the fluid's entering and exit. When the fluid's pressure inside the pipeline is greater than the pressure of flap valve outside the pipeline, the flap valve will automatically open. Otherwise, the flap valve will automatically close and there is no need of gas liquid control or manual control.¬†

2.3.3 Convenient to maintain and long operational life span.
2.4 Range of Application
It is mainly used for flood storage, city self-drainage and inter basin water transfer.
