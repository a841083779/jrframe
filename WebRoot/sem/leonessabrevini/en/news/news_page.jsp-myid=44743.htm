LEONESSAbrevini will take part in BAUMA CHINA 2012

2012-11-05 Click：[318]
Leonessabrevini will attend to Bauma international trade fair scheduled in Shanghai from the 27th to the 30th of November. It is the largest and the most important fair trade in Asia. The company will be located in Hall N2 in the booth N2.111 with samples of its products. In the stand you will find members of our Technical and Commercial departments which will be able to provide to our visitors any kind of informations they need.

Leonessabrevini can provide slewing bearings specifically designed for machinery, construction vehicles, concrete pumps, access platform and even for wind mills industries with an external diameter that starts from 300 mm until 3500 mm; versions with external or internal gear or with no gear at all. in addition, according to the type of load, they are available with 1 row of balls, 2 rows of balls and one row of rollers.

We hope to see you there.
